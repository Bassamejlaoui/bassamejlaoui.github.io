Bassam Mejlaoui

Computational Linguist & Data Scientist

I am a researcher and specialist in Computational Linguistics, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence with advanced studies from Stanford and WorldQuant University. My research focuses on Biomedical Data Science, Quantum NLP, and Financial Technology.

My work spans multiple domains including Neuroinformatics, Brain-Machine Interfaces, Digital Twin Technology, Ethical AI, and Algorithmic Governance, enabling me to develop innovative solutions to complex challenges.

Research Interests

I'm broadly interested in leveraging AI and computational techniques for decision-making and human-machine interaction. My research spans several interconnected disciplines including:

  • Neuroinformatics and Brain-Machine Interfaces
  • Digital Twin Technology
  • Quantum Natural Language Processing
  • Ethical AI and Algorithmic Governance
  • Biomedical Data Science
  • Applied AI and Machine Learning
  • Computational Linguistics and Corpus Linguistics
  • Cryptologic Language Analytics

Featured Research Areas

Quantum NLP

Exploring quantum computing approaches to natural language processing, including quantum embeddings and semantic representations that leverage quantum mechanics principles.

Biomedical Data Science

Developing AI-driven systems for analyzing biomedical data, with a focus on predictive models for diagnostics and treatment optimization.

Ethical AI Systems

Researching frameworks for implementing ethical considerations in AI development, addressing bias, fairness, and accountability in algorithmic decision-making.
